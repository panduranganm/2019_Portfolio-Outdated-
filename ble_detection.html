Summer Research Intership 2019

BLE Detection

This summer, I worked as a research intern on the SubTerranean Team Explorer in Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Lab. My main goal over the summer was to create a localization script that could detect low energy bluetooth devices. I used Python, ROS, and various sensors (BLE detection, audio, geolocation to accomplish this task.

01

I Learned How to:

-Use ROS (Robotics Operating System)
-Implement important robotic principles such as Kalman filters
-Problem solve and troubleshoot software and hardware problems
-Collaborate with different professionals to complete task in timely manner

02

What I Implemented

-Localization script for BLE devices within 10m radius
-Integrated Apriltag detection on robot
-Integrated audio detection on robot
-Handled social media account for SubT Team Explorer
