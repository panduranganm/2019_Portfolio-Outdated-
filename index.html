About Me

Hello!

My name is Maya Pandurangan. I am an undergraduate student at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Intelligent Environments/Design, however, I'm currently in the process of transferring from CS to Informatino Systems with a focus in product design and development. I enjoy exploring the intersection of technology and design and am excited to gain more experience and opportunities to explore.

My core skills include proficiency in programming (Python, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ROS) and strong interpersonal skills. I have experience in product design and working with real-world clients.
